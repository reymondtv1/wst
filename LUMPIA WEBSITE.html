STEPS TO COOK LUMPIA

Ingredients:

    1/2 kg ground pork

    1 cup carrots (finely chopped)

    1 cup cabbage (finely chopped)

    1 onion (finely chopped)

    2 cloves garlic (minced)

    1 egg

    1 tbsp soy sauce

    1 tsp salt

    1/2 tsp ground black pepper

    1 pack lumpia wrappers

    Oil for frying

Instructions:

    In a bowl, mix ground pork, carrots, cabbage, onion, garlic, egg, soy sauce, salt, and black pepper.

    Lay a lumpia wrapper flat and place about 1-2 tablespoons of the mixture in the center.

    Roll the wrapper tightly, folding the sides inward, and seal the edge with water.

    Repeat until all the mixture is used.

    Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.

    Fry lumpia in batches until golden brown and crispy.

    Remove from oil and place on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

    Serve with sweet chili sauce or vinegar dipping sauce. Enjoy!
