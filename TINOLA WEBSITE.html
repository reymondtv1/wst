STEPS TO COOK TINOLA

Ingredients:

    1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)

    5 cloves garlic (minced)

    1 onion (chopped)

    1 thumb-sized ginger (sliced)

    1 tbsp fish sauce (patis)

    4 cups water or chicken broth

    1 green papaya (sliced) or chayote

    1 bunch malunggay leaves or chili leaves

    2 tbsp cooking oil

    Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

    Heat oil in a pot over medium heat.

    Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.

    Add chicken pieces and cook until slightly browned.

    Pour in fish sauce and stir well.

    Add water or chicken broth and bring to a boil.

    Simmer for 30-40 minutes or until the chicken is tender.

    Add sliced papaya or chayote and cook until softened.

    Add malunggay or chili leaves and cook for another 1-2 minutes.

    Season with salt and pepper to taste.

    Turn off the heat and serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
