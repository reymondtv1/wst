STEPS TO COOK PANCIT

Ingredients:

    250g pancit bihon (rice noodles)

    200g chicken breast (sliced)

    1/2 cup shrimp (optional)

    1 cup cabbage (shredded)

    1/2 cup carrots (julienned)

    1/2 cup green beans (sliced)

    1 onion (chopped)

    3 cloves garlic (minced)

    2 tbsp soy sauce

    1 tbsp oyster sauce

    1/2 tsp ground black pepper

    2 cups chicken broth or water

    2 tbsp cooking oil

    Calamansi or lemon for garnish

Instructions:

    Soak pancit bihon noodles in warm water for 10 minutes, then drain and set aside.

    Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.

    Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.

    Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.

    If using shrimp, add and cook for 2 minutes.

    Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, black pepper, and chicken broth. Bring to a boil.

    Add the soaked bihon noodles and mix well until fully absorbed.

    Add cabbage
