STEPS TO COOK HUMBA

Ingredients:

    1 kg pork belly (cut into chunks)

    5 cloves garlic (minced)

    1 onion (chopped)

    ½ cup soy sauce

    ½ cup vinegar

    1 cup pineapple juice

    2 tbsp brown sugar

    1 tsp black peppercorns

    2-3 dried bay leaves

    ½ cup tausi (fermented black beans) (optional)

    1 cup banana blossoms (rinsed and drained) (optional)

    1 cup water

    2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions:

    Heat oil in a pot over medium heat.

    Sauté garlic and onions until fragrant.

    Add the pork belly and cook until lightly browned.

    Pour in soy sauce and vinegar. Do not stir for a few minutes.

    Add pineapple juice, brown sugar, black peppercorns, and bay leaves.

    Pour in water and let it simmer for 1 to 1.5 hours until pork is tender.

    If using tausi and banana blossoms, add them and stir.

    Adjust taste with more sugar, soy sauce, or vinegar if needed.

    Once the sauce thickens and pork is tender, turn off the heat.

    Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
